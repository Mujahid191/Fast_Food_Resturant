Opening Details

Regular Opening Hours

At The Culinary Grill, we're delighted to serve you seven days a week, ensuring that the savory delights of our fast-food haven are always within reach. From Monday through Sunday, our doors swing open at 1:00 PM, inviting you to indulge in the flavors crafted with care and passion. Whether it's a midweek treat or a weekend feast, our regular opening hours from 1:00 PM to 11:00 PM guarantee that you can savor the culinary journey at your convenience.

Special Hours and Holidays

Occasionally, we may spice things up with special hours or celebrate holidays with our valued customers. Keep an eye out for announcements on our website or social media channels for any extended hours during festive seasons or special occasions. While we strive to bring you the joy of fast food every day, we also value the importance of rest and rejuvenation for our team. Any holiday closures will be communicated in advance to ensure you have ample time to plan your visits accordingly. Your understanding and support are appreciated as we continue to curate exceptional culinary experiences for you.
